These little guys are called [kodama](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kodama_%28spirit%29) and were my favorite from [Princess Mononoke](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Princess_Mononoke) made by [Studio Ghibli](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Studio_Ghibli). If you have never seen this film is well worth it.

I need some different color paper! I have run out of everything but black and white. In this one I experimented with reducing the distance between each layer down from 10mm to 5mm. I think it works better.
