## Maps

This batch is all map-adjacent: identifiers, road visualizations, printable terrain, and map-as-art tools.

- [WOEID](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WOEID) - Where On Earth ID. A geographic identifier system that still pops up in older APIs and datasets.

### TrailPrint3D

A generator for 3D printable hiking maps.

- [TrailPrint3D on Printables](https://www.printables.com/model/1242540-hiking-and-cycling-map-generator-trailprint3d-blen)
- [TrailPrint3D on MakerWorld](https://makerworld.com/en/models/1264871-hiking-and-cycling-map-generator-trailprint3d#profileId-1304759)

### Printable maps

- [anvaka/city-roads](https://github.com/anvaka/city-roads) - A road-network visualization tool that turns cities into dense abstract line drawings. These kinds of projects always sit in a nice space between cartography, data viz, and generative art.
- [anvaka/city-roads Vancouver example](https://anvaka.github.io/city-roads/?q=Vancouver%2C%20Canada&areaId=3601852574)
- [PrettyMapp](https://prettymapp.streamlit.app/) - A stylized OpenStreetMap-based poster generator. Clean output
- [Map To {oster](https://github.com/originalankur/maptoposter) - Another map-poster generator. Similar space as PrettyMapp
- [Isometric NYC](https://cannoneyed.com/projects/isometric-nyc) - A beautiful isometric rendering of New York City. Less of a utility and more of an inspiration
