Radiant acrylic mask for #burningman. Mask pattern design by @wintercroft. Made with @vhs #lasercutter. I used the wrong glue (gorilla glue) and it foamed up under the acrylic plates, distorting them. The glue also ate through some of the acrylic foil discoloring it. I'm not happy with the way this turned out and will have to make a new version. 94 of 100 for #the100dayproject. #masks #lowpoly #foxmask #fox #foxy #acrylic
