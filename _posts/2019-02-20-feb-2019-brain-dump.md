## Electronics

- [LEAP](https://leap.tardate.com/): Little Electronic and Arduino Projects - A collection of electronics Projects  many involving an Arduino in one way or another.
- [Powering Digital Addressable RGB LED Pixels](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1t5wzjtVOZT8GrY78y2oB2IpYcejc4ftl9nOms4hBFDU/edit#heading=h.ipphxey3rcdv) - A tips and tricks guild for powering LED projects.

## Projects

- [Guide to building a cnc machine](https://hackaday.com/2018/12/05/the-complete-beginners-guide-to-building-a-cnc-machine/) - A good intro to making a CNC machine using a [Etch A Sketch]((https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Etch_A_Sketch)) 
- [CNC coaster plotter](https://hackaday.com/2018/12/26/make-an-impression-at-the-bar-with-a-cnc-coaster-plotter/) A "Polar" CNC plotter that can draw pictures on to coasters. 
- [Holly shit buttons!](https://twitter.com/codinghorror/status/1081476899937742848/photo/1) - A russian roulette style game where some of the buttons are connected to a bomb. People have to press the buttons and hope they don't explode. 
- [Humans vs. zombies via the ESP8266](https://hackaday.com/2019/02/08/humans-vs-zombies-via-the-esp8266/) - The devices operate using the ESP8266, in AP+station mode. The proximity sensing works on a very simple method. Devices show their human or zombie status by appearing as a WiFi AP by that name, and proximity detection is achieved by showing the signal strength of the opposite AP on an LED bar on the device. Once zombies get close enough to human devices, the humans are infected and become zombies themselves.
- [Retro Time](http://retrotime.org/) - A series of awesome retro projects. Nixie tube, marble mazes, laser cut sculptures, and Automata.
- [Blinkybadge 2017](https://www.crowdsupply.com/sutajio-kosagi/blinkybadge-2017) - A ESP based blinky badge made by [Bunnie Huang](https://www.bunniestudios.com/blog/) for [Burning man](https://burningman.org/).

## Travel

- [Skip Lagged](https://skiplagged.com/) - Shows flights where the layover city is actually where you want to go. Sometimes produces cheaper flights. 
- [Visalist](https://visalist.io/canada/all-visas) - Shows where you can travel with your passport with or without getting a local visa. 
- [SkyScanner](https://www.skyscanner.ca/) - Finds cheap flights with depart and return date ranges. Good for when you are just looking for a trip and you don't know where you want to go.
