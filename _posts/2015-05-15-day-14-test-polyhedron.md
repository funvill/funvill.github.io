This is a test of different [polyhedron shapes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polyhedron). I'm planning on making twelve different shapes. Then cutting patterns into the faces of these shapes and adding LEDs to make lanterns. (similar to the panels for the dome project above). Then hang them all like a chandler or mobile. Eventually these will be made out of laser cut wood.

This shape is a [trapezohedron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trapezohedron)
