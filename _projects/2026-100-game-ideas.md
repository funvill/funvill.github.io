Daily ideas based on a theme chosen by the group.

Some of these ideas are just throw away ideas to satisfy the theme

1. [Seek and Find (Theme: Loop)](/game-idea-1-seek-and-find-theme-loop) - [Demo](/games/grayharbor)
2. [Reverse Katamari Damacy (Theme: Medical Procedures)](/game-idea-2-reverse-katamari-damacy-theme-medical-procedures) - [Demo](/games/reverse-katamari) - Top down vertical scroller,  Reverse “Katamari Damacy” game where you are trying to drag your kid to a doctor's appointment
3. [Necessary (Theme: Medical Procedures)](/game-idea-3-necessary-theme-medical-procedures) - A rhythm game where a strong, tired hand drags a terrified inky mass through the environment, ripping its tendrils free on the beat
4. [Mini Golf 'It can't be this easy' (Theme: It can't be this easy)](/game-idea-4-mini-golf-it-cant-be-this-easy) - A puzzle mini golf game, where every round is a hole-in-one
5. [Trains but in space (Theme: Wormholes/Teleportation)](/game-idea-5-trains-but-in-space-theme-wormholes-teleportation) - Building the galactic wormhole network, one generation ship at a time
6. [One of us! (Theme: Joined Together)](/game-idea-6-one-of-us-theme-joined-together) - Hive mind assimilating earth
7. [Potion maker (Theme: Cooking)](/game-idea-7-potion-maker-theme-cooking) - A dialog driven potion maker game. Listen to customer stories and make judgment calls from incomplete information
8. [New Job for a Kill bot - Story (Theme: Overqualified)](/game-idea-8-new-job-for-a-kill-bot-theme-overqualified) - A fish out of water story: a 10,000 year old AI war machine looks for a new job in a post-scarcity universe
9. [Monster Guess Who - Field Guide Game (Theme: Urban Legends)](/game-idea-9-monster-guess-who-theme-urban-legends) - 'Guess who' of monsters
10. [Thing explain guess - Web game (Theme: The Fablebound Five)](/game-idea-10-thing-explain-guess-theme-the-fablebound-five) - Can you describe complexity with simple words?
11. [DNA Monster Hunter (Theme: Contemporary Epic Fantasy)](/game-idea-11-dna-monster-hunter-theme-contemporary-epic-fantasy) - Hunt monsters, take their genes, make new monsters, repeat
12. [Tiny machines (Theme: Small, Micro, Minuscule, Teeny, Tiny, Itty-bitty, Infinitesimal)](/game-idea-12-tiny-machines-theme-small) - Tiny racing machines, in an oversized world
13. [Jazzy vibes (Theme: Jack Box Games #1)](/game-idea-13-jazzy-vibes-theme-jack-box-games) - Vibe out with your fiends
14. [Road Trip Radio (Theme: Road trip)](/game-idea-14-road-trip-radio-theme-road-trip) - Mini game based around who gets to choose the radio station on a road trip
15. [Road Trip Map (Theme: Road trip)](/game-idea-15-road-trip-map-theme-road-trip) - The best road trip with your friends
16. [Expanding Body Platformer (Theme: Body Horror)](/game-idea-16-expanding-body-platformer-theme-body-horror) - A puzzle platformer with a snake style expanding body. Eat enemies to gain their powers
17. [Detective (Theme: Detective)](/game-idea-17-detective-theme-detective) - You're a detective hunting a Time Looper bomber
18. [The symptoms (Theme: A mysterious illness)](/game-idea-18-the-symptoms-theme-a-mysterious-illness) - Fool the doctor into giving you a sick note. A Fibbage style bluffing game of fake symptoms
19. [Pooper scrolls (Theme: A mysterious illness)](/game-idea-19-pooper-scrolls-theme-a-mysterious-illness) - A man discovers his stool samples contain embroidered scrolls that accurately predict his future
20. ⭐ [Good taste (Theme: Otherworldly Chef)](/game-idea-20-good-taste-theme-otherworldly-chef) - A puzzle game where you are a lifeform building up your senses from scratch, until you can cook a meal for an otherworldly being
21. [Cocktail party (Theme: A Strange Fetish)](/game-idea-21-cocktail-party-theme-a-strange-fetish) - Talk to NPCs, use 'magnetic poetry' to build a strange fetish, entice the most NPCs. Social game
22. [AI Aztecs (Theme: What is in that mysterious black box of Doom?)](/game-idea-22-ai-aztecs-theme-mysterious-black-box-of-doom) - A galactic religion feeds human consciousness into an AI black box of Doom in exchange for technology
23. [One wish (Theme: Cryptozoology)](/game-idea-23-one-wish-theme-cryptozoology) - Every child in the world gets one wish. Sometimes that wish creates a monster
24. [Child Actor (Theme: What's next after the hero's journey?)](/game-idea-24-child-actor-theme-after-the-heros-journey) - An interview with the washed up hero who publicly saved the world from an evil most foul at sixteen
25. ⭐ [Flowers shop (Theme: Unsettlingly wholesome)](/game-idea-25-flowers-shop-theme-unsettlingly-wholesome) - Sell flowers to townsfolk who love you, while harvesting the flowers from the town. (AKA cute drug dealer with flowers)
26. [Robot (Theme: Something that takes 10 years of daily work to do)](/game-idea-26-robot-theme-ten-years-of-daily-work) - A robot crossing the country over 10 years, controlled by daily votes from Patreon contributors
27. [Regrets (Theme: Trashy Horror)](/game-idea-27-regrets-theme-trashy-horror) - A horror story of forced backwards time travel in a fated universe
28. [Bird Hunt (Theme: Lens or eye piece or sight)](/game-idea-28-bird-hunt-theme-lens-or-eye-piece-or-sight) - Make a game out of the frustrations of using a telephoto lens while trying to photograph small song birds
29. ⭐ [The old man's magic (Theme: Elderly people with super powers)](/game-idea-29-the-old-mans-magic-theme-elderly-people-with-super-powers) - The last magic user in the world has lived too long. A hero's journey to defeat him and release magic back into the world
30. ⭐ [The perfect cup of coffee (Theme: Precursor or The perfect cup of coffee)](/game-idea-30-the-perfect-cup-of-coffee-theme-precursor) - Speed run humanity's progress towards coffee
31. [Scale (Theme: Science Fiction Horror)](/game-idea-31-scale-theme-science-fiction-horror) - A systemic eco-horror where two identical Earths destroy each other through convenience and exploitation
32. [Local choir (Theme: Hello sir, would you like to join our cult?)](/game-idea-32-local-choir-theme-join-our-cult) - You joined a local choir to meet new people. It's a cult
33. [Consensu (Theme: Technically Children's Content, but Would be Banned in Many Jurisdictions)](/game-idea-33-consensu-theme-technically-childrens-content) - A Jack Box style voting game that punishes divergence and teaches you to vote with the group
